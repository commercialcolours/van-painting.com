Van painting

When your van needs painting or repairing after an accident, how do you choose a company to return it to its original condition? What should you look for when selecting a van and LGV accident repairs company?

  • They should cater for lighter commercial vehicles, from car-derived vans up to large vans with long wheel-bases and high roofs.
  • They repair minor dents and scrapes, carry out refurbishments, and heavy smash repairs.
  • They can use your paint-scheme or company colours.
  • They can help design new paint-schemes and graphics.
  • They have sign-writers who can cut and apply silhouettes.

An van and LGV accident repairs company should cater for all types of commercial vehicles.

Professional van painting company

A professional van painting and repair company should offer all types of paint finishes and use only high-quality products. They should use computerised paint mixing systems like PPG and Glasurit which produce exact colour matches, and their environment should be dust free, which will provide a high quality finish.
